Our Story

How four CSE students came together to build a mental wellness platform.

2024

The Beginning

As Computer Science students at Sonargaon University, we recognized the growing need for accessible mental health support among students and young adults. We decided to use our technical skills to make a difference.

2024

Research & Development

We conducted extensive research on mental health challenges, interviewed healthcare professionals, and studied existing platforms to understand the gaps in mental health support systems.

2024

Platform Launch

After months of development, testing, and refinement, we launched the Mental Wellness Consultation Platform - a comprehensive solution for mental health support.

Present

Continuous Improvement

We continue to enhance the platform based on user feedback, adding new features and improving the user experience to better serve our community.
