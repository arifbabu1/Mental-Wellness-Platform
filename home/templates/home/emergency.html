Simple Steps to Handle Crisis

Follow these steps when you're in crisis. Remember, this feeling will pass.

1

STOP

  • Don't act
  • Wait 10 minutes
  • Take slow deep breaths
⏸️
2

STAY SAFE

  • Remove harmful things
  • Don't stay alone
  • Go to a safe place
🛡️
3

THINK AGAIN

  • Ask: "Is this really true?"
  • Thoughts are not facts
🧠
4

TALK TO SOMEONE

  • Call or message a friend/family
  • Or call a helpline
👥
5

TAKE CARE OF BODY

  • Sleep
  • Eat
  • Walk outside
🏃‍♂️
6

GET HELP

  • Talk to a doctor or therapist
🏥
7

MAKE A PLAN

  • Who to call
  • Where to go
  • What helps you calm down
📋
